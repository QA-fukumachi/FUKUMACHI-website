BIOGRAPHY

Fukumachi is a Georgian visionary artist born and raised in Tbilisi. His creative path began with painting and a deep connection to music — from jazz and hip-hop to Georgian folk. A turning point came when he discovered flamenco guitar, which reshaped his understanding of music and expression. For him, flamenco represents both the origin and the essence of sound itself.

After years of guitar playing and experimentation, he found his voice in electronic music. What started as imitation gradually evolved into a mature, distinctive sound. His journey naturally transitioned from house to electro and techno, eventually expanding into ambient territories. Today, Fukumachi moves fluidly across the wide spectrum of techno, blending past influences into a sound that is unpredictable, limitless, and deeply immersive.

Beyond genre labels, he approaches creativity as a multi-dimensional force — shaped by people, experiences, and moments of inspiration. Understanding that inspiration cannot be forced, he embraces its rhythm, allowing each phase of his artistic evolution to unfold with honesty and intention.
